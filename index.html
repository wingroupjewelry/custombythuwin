The Journey

Custom Jewelry
Timeline

Every masterpiece follows a thoughtful process — from the first dream to the moment you hold your treasure.

Step 01

Free Consultation

We begin with a complimentary conversation to understand your vision, occasion, style preferences, and budget. No commitment needed — just bring your ideas and inspiration.

Day 1
Step 02

Design & Concept

Our designer translates your ideas into detailed sketches and 3D CAD renderings. You'll see exactly how your piece will look before a single gram of gold is touched.

Days 2–5
Step 03

Material Selection

Choose your gold purity, metal type, and diamonds or gemstones. We source only conflict-free, certified stones and present options that suit your vision and budget perfectly.

Days 5–7
Step 04

Crafting & Setting

Master goldsmiths bring your design to life — casting, hand-finishing, and setting every stone with precision. Each step is photographed so you can follow the journey.

Weeks 1–2
Step 05

Quality Inspection

Before delivery, every piece undergoes a rigorous quality inspection to ensure it meets our exacting luxury standards. Certificates are prepared for diamonds and precious metals.

Weeks 2–3
Step 06

Delivery & Reveal

Your treasure arrives in a signature Custom By Thu Win Luxury Box, ready to be gifted or worn with our complimentary FedEx Overnight Insured Package. We also offer a free resizing or adjustment within 30 days.

Weeks 2–3
